summary: Trade attack accuracy for a surge of melee damage.
meta:
  - Combat
  - Melee

You can choose to take a **–1 penalty** on all melee attack rolls and combat
maneuver checks to gain a **+2 bonus** on all melee damage rolls. This bonus to
damage is increased by half (+50%) for a two-handed weapon.

When your base attack bonus reaches +4, and every +4 thereafter, the penalty and
bonus both increase.

| BAB | Attack penalty | Damage bonus (1H) | Damage bonus (2H) |
|-----|----------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| +1  | -1 | +2 | +3 |
| +4  | -2 | +4 | +6 |
| +8  | -3 | +6 | +9 |
| +12 | -4 | +8 | +12 |
