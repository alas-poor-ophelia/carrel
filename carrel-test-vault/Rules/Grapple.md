type: process
category: Maneuver
summary: Seize and restrain a foe through a sequence of combat-maneuver checks.
meta:
  - CMB
  - Standard
  - Melee
---

As a standard action, make a combat maneuver check (CMB) against the target's CMD.

1. **Initiate** — make a CMB check vs. the target's CMD. Success makes both creatures grappled.
2. **Maintain** — on later turns, make another CMB check as a standard action to keep the grapple and gain a benefit.
3. **Move** — move both creatures up to half your speed (a successful check).
4. **Damage** — deal damage equal to an unarmed strike.
5. **Pin** — make the target helpless until you stop pinning.

> A grappled creature takes a –4 penalty to Dexterity and a –2 penalty on attacks
> and most checks, and cannot move or take actions that require two hands.
