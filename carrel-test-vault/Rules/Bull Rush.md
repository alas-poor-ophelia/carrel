---
type: process
category: Maneuver
summary: Shove an opponent straight back without provoking when you have the feat.
meta:
  - CMB
  - Standard
---

1. Provoke an attack of opportunity unless you have **Improved Bull Rush**.
2. Make a combat maneuver check vs. the target's CMD.
3. On success, push the target back 5 feet; each 5 by which you beat the CMD pushes 5 feet more.
4. You can move with the target if you have room.
