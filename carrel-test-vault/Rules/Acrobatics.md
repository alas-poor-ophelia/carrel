Use **Acrobatics** to keep your balance, tumble past enemies, and reduce falling damage.

- **Move through a threatened square** — DC equals 10 + the opponent's CMD.
- **Move through an enemy's space** — DC equals 5 + the opponent's CMD.
- **Balance on a narrow surface** — DC by surface width (7"–12" wide is DC 10).
- **Reduce falling damage** — DC 15 to ignore the first 10 feet fallen.
